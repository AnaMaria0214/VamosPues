    • GUATAPE

    Guatapé, a charming town nestled in the heart of Eastern Antioquia, captivates visitors with its unique blend of history, natural splendor, and a vibrant culture. Renowned for the imposing "Piedra del Peñol" and the picturesque streets adorned with colorful baseboards, it offers an unforgettable experience. Its historical legacy dates back to the pre-Columbian Tahamíes civilization, enduring Spanish colonization and undergoing various transformations over time, from political conflicts to agricultural and touristic development.

    The Guatapé Reservoir, also known as the Peñol-Guatapé Reservoir, is a wonder that beautifies the region with a fascinating history. Conceived in the 1970s as part of a hydroelectric project, the construction of the reservoir involved flooding a valley, displacing entire communities. Despite this, it became a crucial tourist attraction, offering natural beauty, water activities, and an impressive landscape with hills and mountains emerging from the water, creating an atmosphere of serenity and wonder.
